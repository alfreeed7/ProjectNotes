Design Choices

Our CSS is a dark, world travel themed design. We chose to go with a subtle theme, making use of dark blues and purples as our base colors and lighter blues and white as highlights and text color. Also worth noting is the fact that we changed the opacity of the day box backgrounds for aesthetic purposes. This We also made it a goal to center elements wherever practical. For accentuating details, we included images that reinforce the travel theme, such as the header icons and the passport icon next to the profile picture. Our choice of icons in the day boxes is also signficant. For past days, we used a mountain with a flag planted on it, showing that we have completed days. The current day has a road, symbolizing a journey, and future days have a globe with a flight path, symbolizing that it is our destination.

Our design includes multiple interactive features. Day boxes expand when the user hovers over them, and the header fades away as the user scrolls down. Furthrermore, the links in the header and the day boxes both have their own unique effect when the user hovers over them.
