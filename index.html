Design Choices

Our CSS is a dark, world travel themed design. We chose to go with a subtle theme, making use of dark blues and purples as our base colors and lighter blues and white as highlights and text color. We also made it a goal to center elements wherever practical. For accentuating details, we included images that reinforce the travel theme, such as the header icons and the passport icon next to the profile picture.
